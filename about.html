Introduction

PERSATUAN KEBAJIKAN RUMAH VICTORY MALAYSIA [PPM-012-10-02051997]

Since 1988

Rumah Victory is a non-profit charitable organization founded by the late Mr. Philip Mok Sew Kwong in 1988. It began with Christian Drug Rehabilitation Centre and later expanded to Children and Youth Home and Elderly Home. At present, we have:

  • Drug Rehabilitation Centre
  • Children and Youth Home
  • Elderly Home

Purpose

  • To help drug addicts overcome their addiction and to provide life-changing training for them to reintegrate into the community.
  • To provide a shelter home for socially vulnerable groups, such as drug addicts, children from single-parent and broken families, orphans, marginal juveniles, abandoned and sick old folks.

Mission

  • To provide life-changing training programmes for drug addicts to reintegrate into the community, to glorify God, and benefit the people.
  • To show the love and care of Christ by providing counseling and teaching them the truth of the Bible so that they may find new hope in life.

Vision

  • To set up centers in each and every state in East and West Malaysia, serving the community.
  • To build “My Dream Home” - a combination of the Drug Rehabilitation Centre, Children and Youth Home, and Elderly Home.

Founder of Rumah Victory - The Late Mr. Philip Mok Sew Kwong

One of the main reasons for establishing this Rehabilitation Center is to give shelter to the homeless. The word “home” has been a strong emphasis in our old folk center and children center.

As a child, I had a home, but I could not find security and warmth in that place. As a result, I became a drug addict. I used to steal and rob to sustain my lifestyle. However, I always remembered my real home. I would bring money that I robbed to give to my siblings.

After completing a rehabilitation program, the concept of home was always dear to my heart. God gave me a vision and a burden to start a home for the needy. A home is not just a building; it is a place where you can experience victory, be transformed, and stand strong again.

Our society has become more complicated. The vision for “RUMAH VICTORY Children home” is to teach children the right values of life and to mentor and groom them for the future.

Chairman of Rumah Victory

Pastor Ong Kah Hoo

As leaders, we must seek growth every day. Growth defines who we become tomorrow. With God, all things are possible.

Rumah Victory Director General (Central Region)

Mr. Pang Foo Wen

Rumah Victory has gone through many challenges since its incorporation. The founder, Mr. Philip Mok, initiated this and persevered through until today. We hope to build an integrated Rumah Victory that can house a rehabilitation center, an elderly home, and a children and youth home.
