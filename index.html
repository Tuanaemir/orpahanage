Rumah Victory

Rumah Victory is a non-profit charitable organization founded by the late Mr. Philip Mok Sew Kwong in 1988. It began with a Christian Drug Rehabilitation Centre and expanded to include a Children and Youth Home and an Elderly Home, all established to provide community services.
